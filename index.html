05. Serbia Forest Tour

The most common trees in Serbia are oak, beech, pines, and firs. In the Banjica forest, common trees include pedunculate oak, red maple, silver maple, and box elder.Broad-leaved forests, which are mostly oak and beech, cover up to one-third of Serbia. In mountainous areas, trees cover two-fifths or more of the territory.
